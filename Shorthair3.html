Siamese

"The Siamese cat is one of the most recognizable and popular breeds, known for its sleek, slender body, large almond-shaped blue eyes, and striking color contrast between its light-colored body and darker points on the ears, face, paws, and tail. Siamese cats are highly social, vocal, and affectionate, often forming strong bonds with their human companions. They are intelligent and curious, enjoying interactive play and attention. Known for their vocal nature, they often 'talk' to their owners, making them an engaging and loyal pet. Their playful, energetic, and loving personality makes them a favorite among cat lovers."
