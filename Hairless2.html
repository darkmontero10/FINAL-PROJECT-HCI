Bambino

"The Bambino cat is a rare and unique breed that combines the hairlessness of the Sphynx with the short legs of the Munchkin. This gives the Bambino a distinctive, almost kitten-like appearance throughout its life. Despite their small stature, Bambinos are energetic, playful, and affectionate. They are known for being outgoing and social, often following their humans around and seeking cuddles. Their lack of fur means they require special care to stay warm and clean, but their loving personality and adorable looks make them a cherished companion for those who enjoy interactive and unusual pets."
